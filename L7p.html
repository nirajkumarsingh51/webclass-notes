Introduction:

Initially, blockchain is only used as a foundation of cryptocurrency, but today, we can see the rise of this new emerging technology are being implemented in many industries. In the future, most technologies around the world are expected to use blockchain as an efficient way to make online transactions. One of the areas that blockchain technologies can be applied crowdfunding platforms. The most common problem with the current crowdfunding scene around the world is that the campaigns are not regulated and some of the crowd-funding campaigns turned out to be fraud. Besides, the completion of some projects was also significantly delayed. This project aims to solve these problems by applying Ethereum smart contracts to the crowdfunding site to that the contracts will be fully automatically executed, thus preventing frauds and ensuring that the projects can be delivered within duration given.



Project Statement:

A University wants to set up a new lab for its students. for this approached to student's parents asking them to contribute to gathering funds for setup the new computer lab
In a rapidly evolving digital landscape, crowdfunding has emerged as a powerful tool for individuals and organizations to raise funds for their innovative ideas, charitable causes, and entrepreneurial ventures. However, traditional crowdfunding platforms often suffer from issues related to transparency, accountability, and trust.
To address these challenges, a new crowdfunding platform is being developed that leverages the potential of blockchain technology to create a secure, efficient, and decentralized ecosystem for crowdfunding campaigns.
In the fast-paced digital world, crowdfunding has become a potent means for gathering funds to support various ventures, including educational projects like the University's new computer lab. However, traditional crowdfunding platforms may encounter challenges concerning transparency, accountability, and trust. To overcome these obstacles and revolutionize the fundraising process, the University is proud to introduce a cutting-edge solution - its own decentralized crowdfunding platform, powered by blockchain technology.
The University's new crowdfunding platform is designed to offer a secure, efficient, and decentralized ecosystem for raising funds to set up a state-of-the- art computer lab. Embracing the potential of blockchain technology, this platform aims to transform the way contributions are gathered and managed. By leveraging blockchain's transparency and immutability, the University seeks to enhance trust and accountability among donors, ensuring that their valuable contributions are utilized effectively.

There are several areas where block-chain supports and improves crowdfunding, crowdfunding platforms powered by blockchain technology removes the need for intermediate third party.

  • Universal Opportunity: Any project using a block-chain-based crowdfunding model can get funded. Any person with an internet connection can contribute projects.

Key Advantages:

  1. Transparency:- The blockchain-based platform ensures that all transactions and fund utilization are recorded on an immutable ledger. This level of transparency fosters trust among contributors as they can verify how their funds are utilized throughout the process.
  2. Security:- Blockchain's cryptographic features guarantee the secure storage of donors' financial information, reducing the risk of data breaches and fraud.
  3. Decentralization:- The crowdfunding platform operates on a decentralized network of nodes, ensuring that no single entity has absolute control. This enhances security and reduces the risk of central point failures.
